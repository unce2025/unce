International Shipping Tips

How to avoid common pitfalls when shipping across borders

Posted on March 1, 2025 by UNCE Editorial Team

Shipping internationally for the first time? There are several important factors to consider before sending your cargo overseas. At United Nation Cargo Express, we simplify the process and help you avoid costly mistakes.

International Shipping Checklist

  • Check customs regulations for your destination country
  • Ensure documentation is complete and accurate
  • Label all packages clearly with international standards
  • Choose reliable shipping partners with customs experience

Use our Request a Quote tool to get precise shipping rates. Understanding all costs ahead of time prevents surprises, especially with duties and taxes.

Customs and Documentation

Missing or incorrect customs documents are the #1 cause of international shipping delays. Our team ensures your customs clearance is smooth and compliant with local regulations.

Use Professional Guidance

Our logistics professionals are here to help with packaging tips, carrier selection, and tracking support. We turn international shipping into a smooth, stress-free experience.
