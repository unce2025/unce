Choosing the Right Freight Option

Understanding the differences between air and ocean shipping

Posted on April 12, 2025 by UNCE Editorial Team

When shipping internationally, choosing between air freight and ocean freight can significantly affect your delivery time, budget, and efficiency. Each option comes with its own advantages depending on the nature of your shipment.

Key Differences

  • Air Freight: Best for urgent, high-value, or lightweight shipments.
  • Ocean Freight: Ideal for bulk goods or large volumes that are less time-sensitive.

What to Consider

At United Nation Cargo Express, we help you decide based on:

  • Delivery speed requirements
  • Cost considerations
  • Type and size of cargo
  • Destination regulations and logistics
